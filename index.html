POWER POP BUBBLESLior2020-01-13T14:11:29+00:00
Power Pop Bubbles reached top ranks in Google Play App Store in more than 16 countries. Power Pop Bubbles is another fan-favorite game with thousands of fun puzzle levels that offers a modern twist to the classic Bubble Shooter gameplay. Players can enjoy a cutting-edge design and a variety of exciting new elements and effects. Your goal is to match 3 or more bubbles of the same color to create a line blast and score points. Enjoy the awesome Super Aim feature that will help you to take accurate shots and pop your way around different challenges!
